We Turn Complaints Into Action

We are a community-driven platform dedicated to improving road safety through efficient pothole detection and repair prioritization. Our mission is to empower citizens to actively participate in maintaining safe road infrastructure while providing local authorities with data-driven insights for optimal resource allocation.

Our platform bridges the gap between concerned citizens and municipal authorities by combining cutting-edge machine learning technology with community engagement. We believe that road maintenance should be transparent, efficient, and responsive to community needs.

Founded on the principles of civic responsibility and technological innovation, our platform transforms the traditional complaint system into a collaborative ecosystem where every report matters and every voice counts.

Fixing roads isn't just maintenance—it's a step towards restoring public confidence and improving the quality of life. The road to a better India is paved with better roads.

Our team consists of passionate engineers, data scientists, and civic-minded professionals committed to creating safer roads and stronger communities through technology and citizen participation.
